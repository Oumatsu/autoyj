Privacy Policy.

Last Updated: October 2026

At autoYJ ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal and corporate information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit our website (autoyj.com) or interact with our B2B procurement services.

1. Information We Collect

As a B2B export facilitator, we collect information primarily to qualify leads and facilitate international trade. We may collect:

  • Corporate Identity Data: Company name, dealership size, target African market, and business registration details (when requested).
  • Contact Data: Full name, professional email address, and WhatsApp/phone numbers.
  • Procurement Data: Estimated monthly volume, preferred vehicle models (e.g., LFP vs. NCM preferences), and specific trade terms (CIF, FOB).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and interaction metrics gathered via standard analytics cookies to optimize our website performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data collected exclusively for B2B operational purposes:

  • To provide accurate wholesale matrix pricing and customized shipping quotes.
  • To contact you via WhatsApp or Email regarding your Deal Room application.
  • To distribute requested technical playbooks, PDI checklists, and market insights.
  • To comply with international export regulations and KYC (Know Your Customer) protocols.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your data. We treat all dealership metrics and procurement volumes with strict confidentiality. We only share necessary information with verified third-party partners involved in fulfilling your order:

  • Logistics & Freight Forwarders: (e.g., Maersk, COSCO) for booking RoRo or container slots.
  • Customs Brokers: For preparing Bill of Lading, Certificate of Origin, and battery safety certificates.

4. Data Security

We implement bank-grade administrative and technical security measures to protect your corporate data. However, please note that no electronic transmission over the internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. Financial transactions (30/70 T/T or L/C) are handled via verified international banking channels, not directly through this website.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, ask for corrections, or request deletion of your data from our marketing/CRM databases. To exercise these rights, please contact our compliance desk at legal@autoyj.com.
